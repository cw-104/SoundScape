What are the caveats of our models?

Language

Our model is trained on english audio only, this means that non English audio will produce unreliable results. It is only recommended to use our sevice for songs in English.

Trained on Music

Our system is focused on deepfake music. As such our models have not been evaluated on accuracy against spoken DeepFakes. It is not recommended to use this software to detect spoken audio. The quality of spoken DeepFake detection models is far higher, and it is an area that massive tech companies have invested more resources than we could match in solving. Our focus is on music where fewer good solutions exist.

General AI Caveat

No AI is perfect, we worked hard to make a system that is as accurate as possible. We strived to improve accuracy in an area of DeepFake detection that is under-research and with poor real-world results. Our performance is a great improvement, but nonetheless our results are never perfect. This is a tool to aide in detection and should not be treated as a whole solution.

Why Is This Important?



AI is not perfect. We believe this needs to be more transparent to users in every application and industry. AI can be extremely useful, but it also can and has caused massive issues. AI is a tool, not a replacement for real research and work. We want users to approach AI with and understanding of the shortcomings, risks and understanding that AI information needs to be fact checked. AI is not a replacement for human work, it is a tool to aide and hopefully improve our work.
