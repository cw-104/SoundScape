Methods

We Utilize multiple models that together give us a better result more resistant to false positives. We weight finetuned variants of each model based on their strengths. The goal of our algorithm is to lean towards a file being authentic, each model will give a vote towards whether it thinks a file is real or not. The models are adjust based on certainty metrics that are outputted alongside the guessed label to make higher the bar of certainty towards a file being fake

Confidence is calculated by averaging scores given to us by each model and converted into a metric of low medium and high.
