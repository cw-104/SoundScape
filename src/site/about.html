Our Methods

Detection Techniques

We utilize advanced machine learning algorithms to analyze audio analyze deepfake detection. We utilize 5 models (which you can find in our credits page) which each pass over a given audio file in both its original form, as well an isolated sample of only vocals from the file. For some of our models we have even trained twice, we use a variant trained on original audio when we pass over the original, and a model trained on isolated audio when we do the vocal pass. The combination of these models allows us to properly "fact check" results. Instead of relying on one model to be perfect, we use multiple models that we finetune to their best forms then weight them as part of a larger set.

We also care a lot about the problem of false positves, this means Real audio classified incorrectly as Fake. This is a big danger in AI as it leads to overuse of AI. Overuse of AI has lead to implementation of AI in places that are at best unnecessary and decreasing quality and suppliying misleading information. We want to give the best results we can while minimizing the risk of calling real sample fake, undermining artists.

Model Training

We implemented well-researched and high performance models, finetuned on a dataset of our creation with much more relevant and diverse samples. Further we trained each model twice, on isolated vocals and on original audio resulting in two variants of each model. 3/5 of our models showed improvement in their respective trained category allowing us to use those specified trained variants on the proper state of the audio (vocals / original â€“ backing track and all). Two of our models performed strongly when applied to both vocals and original, and the two separate trains did not show signifcant differences leading us to use the one best model for both audio forms.

Team SoundScape: A Generative AI Artist Detection Program


Read Our Paper

This paper presents SoundScape, an innovatice approach to deepfake audio detection, focusing on the music industry. Our system uses vocal isolation to enhance detection accuracy, separating vocals from backing tracks. This improves input quality for our model, which utilizes various deepfake detection programs, resulting in a more precise analysis. Addressing the growing threat of deepfake media, our solution protects artists and celebrities by verifying audio authenticity. We offer a user-friendly web interface for non-technical users to upload files, track their analysis, and receive detailed results. Emphasizing transparency, our model provides a percentage likelihood of a deepfake and the reasoning behind it, reducing false positive outputs. By continuously expanding our dataset and retraining our AI model, we ensure effectiveness against the best and latest deepfake methods. This paper explores deepfake technology, its legal and ethical challenges, and our proposed solution for detecting deepfake audio, contributing to the ongoing debate of regulating and mitigating deepfake risks.

Results

In our evaluation we detect about 72% of deepfakes correctly, and 83% of Real Audio correctly Classified. & 61% of Deepfake Audio correctly classified.

It is important to us that we classify reals as correctly as possible. False positives are audio sample that we classify as DeepFake, but they are actually Real.
